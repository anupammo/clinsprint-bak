Workshops And Events

ClinSprint workshop and events shall open opportunities for further learning. We conduct customized workshops, events and clinical research trainings for educational institutions and corporates. Our main focus is to get the participants involve in activities rather than sitting and listening to experts talk about it and we intend to work with corporates, educational institutions, not-for-profit organizations and think tanks. Participants actively learn by doing – individually and collectively.The workshop’s methods and tools are designed to be applied in real time,easily retained and used in a sustainable clinical trials practice.Our experiential workshop techniques foster curiosity, exploration, embracing different perspectives and then applying new thinking in real time.

About Sub-heading

We do also conduct on-site clinical research training programs which come in many formats. They can run as short as 2 hours to as long as several days.

Please contact us and we can tailor workshops and events to suit your group’s specific needs and interests.
